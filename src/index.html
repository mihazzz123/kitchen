Kitchen Remodeling

In recent years, home design has shifted with a heavier emphasis on the kitchen as the focal point. The kitchen is no longer a place solely for cooking, but also a gathering space for eating, socializing, and entertaining. If your kitchen hasn’t evolved with this societal shift, it may be time for a remodel, and luckily, it’s often feasible to transform a closed off, out-dated kitchen into an open-concept showstopper.

Do you have house envy because the one you live in is outdated? Older homes are configured much differently than today’s open floor plans. At the turn of the century, houses were built with multiple single-purpose rooms – formal dining rooms included. Master suites are a relatively new concept because houses seldom had more than one bathroom and people had much smaller wardrobes than we do today.
